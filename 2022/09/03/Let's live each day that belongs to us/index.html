Let's live each day that belongs to us

Created2022-09-03|Updated2022-09-03|Everyday life
|Word count:308|Reading time:1min|Post View:

English

Life is a rhythm

There must be light and shadow

Left is right

Have a sunny with snow

Taste is contained in this fierce and constant twists and turns

And the world we live in

It’s more like a cycle

In the time when it’s not being noticed

In the change of seasons

In the stars where the sun rises and the moon sets

If every corner of the earth were filled with light

Who needs stars

Who would look away at night

Looking for distant security

When tomorrow is today

Today is yesterday

And finally become a day that no longer matters in my memory

Don’t talk about how things have changed

Time always learns not to wait

There is only now to cherish

Chinese

生活是种律动

须有光有影

有左有右

有晴有雪

滋味就含在这猛而不变的曲折里

而我们生活的世界

更像是一种轮回

在不被注意的时间里

在四季的变化中

也在这日升月落的繁星里

如果大地的每个角落都充满了光明

谁还需要星星

谁还会在夜里遥望

寻找遥远的安稳

当明天变成今天

今天成为昨天

最后成为记忆里不再重要的一天

不要再谈事过境迁

时间它始终学不会等待

能珍惜的只有现在​

End

Let’s call it a day and wish you a better day tomorrow

Good night
