今日份短诗 (2022.09.04)

Created2022-09-04|Updated2022-09-04|Everyday life
|Word count:333|Reading time:1min|Post View:

今日份短诗

  • 别让现实卷走希望
  • 每个人都会遇到不可逃避的成长
  • 也会经历现实夺走天真
  • 但是他奈何不了你
  • 只会让你更加强大
  • 许多人所谓的成熟
  • 不过是被习惯磨去了棱角
  • 变的世故而实际 那不是成熟
  • 而是精神的早衰和个性的夭亡
  • 真正的成熟 应当是独特个性的形成
  • 真实自我的发现 精神上的结果与丰收
  • 命运是勇敢者的游戏
  • 苦难是人生的一种财富
  • ​世界以痛吻​我
  • 我将报之以歌
  • 加油行路人​
  • Don’t let reality drown out hope
  • Growth is inevitable for everyone
  • And experience reality taking away innocence
  • But he can’t touch you
  • It only makes you stronger
  • What many people call maturity
  • But habit has taken the edge off
  • Becoming sophisticated when actually that’s not growing up
  • but premature decay of spirit and premature death of character
  • True maturity should be the formation of a unique personality
  • The discovery of the true self Spiritual results and harvest
  • Destiny is a game for the brave
  • Suffering is a kind of wealth in life
  • The world kissed me with pain
  • I’ll reply with a song
  • Go for it

结语

每个人都会遇到不可逃避的成长，也会经历现实夺走纯真，但是它只会让你变得更加强大！加油！

夜已深了，晚安，行路人

Good night, Tomorrow will be better, It will be.
